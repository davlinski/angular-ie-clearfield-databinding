Do try with IE 10 or newer !

Type some content in the input box. Then click on the clear field button by hovering the field with your mouse.

Without angular-ie-clearfield-databinding

When cleared, the data binding won't update the template value underneath the input.

[type text] [type search]
myValue1: {{myValue1}}

With angular-ie-clearfield-databinding

When cleared, the data binding will occur and the template value underneath the input is updated.

[type text] [type search]
myValue2: {{myValue2}}
